TASK NEST

Welcome to my To-Do List application! This simple yet powerful tool is designed to help you stay organized and boost productivity. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this app lets you easily add tasks, mark them as complete, and remove them when you're done.


  • Key Features:
    • Task Management: Quickly add new tasks, check off completed ones, and delete tasks you no longer need
    • Light/Dark Mode: A theme toggle lets you switch between light and dark modes, giving you control over your app's appearance based on your preference.
    • Responsive Design: The app is fully responsive, meaning it works beautifully across all devices, from desktops to smartphones.
    • Persistent Data: Your tasks are stored in your browser's localStorage, so they remain even after you refresh or close the page.
